Frequently Asked Questions

What is the total investment required?

The total investment ranges from $250,000 to $500,000 depending on location, size, and build-out requirements. This includes franchise fee, equipment, initial inventory, and working capital.

How much training is provided?

We provide 4 weeks of comprehensive training at our headquarters plus 2 weeks of on-site training before opening. Ongoing training is available as needed.

What ongoing fees are involved?

The ongoing fees include a 6% royalty fee on gross sales and a 2% marketing fee. These fees fund ongoing support, research and development, and national marketing campaigns.

How long does it take to open a franchise?

From signing the franchise agreement to opening day typically takes 6-9 months. This includes site selection, build-out, training, and hiring.

Do you help with financing?

While we don't provide direct financing, we have relationships with several lenders familiar with our brand and can provide guidance on financing options.
